EUROCOPTER ASTAR

The Eurocopter AStar offers a large, comfortable cabin with all forward facing seats and a fast, quiet, smooth flight. The aircraft seats five passengers plus one pilot. This aircraft is excellent for executive charter, aerial survey and sightseeing. This aircraft is also ideal for aerial cinematography. The AStar is the most capable, user-friendly helicopter in its class.
