FAQ

1. Can I fly myself?

No, unless you have a private pilot license.

2. Can I refund my order(s)?

Yes, but only if you cancel a week before the flight. No refunds available on the day of the flight.

3. How should I pay?

You can pay in cash, credit cards, direct deposits and even in gold bricks.

4. Will it be scarry, it's my first time?

Our pilots are well trained and guaranteed that ride will be as smooth as possible. Barf bags are available.

5. Can I bring pets?

No, They are too hairy to carry on board.
