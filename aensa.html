Experienced Flight Instructors.

Our flight instructors are also corporate pilots, and in addition to giving flight instruction, they perform charters, photo and filming flights, rescue flights and many other types of flying missions. As a AENSA student, you will receive the benefit of our pilots' experience in the industry.

One-on-One personalized ground and flight instruction and flexible schedules.

Students meet with their instructor on a one-on-one basis and are thereby able to progress at their own pace. A post-flight debriefing to review and re-emphasize the day's lesson follows each flight.
